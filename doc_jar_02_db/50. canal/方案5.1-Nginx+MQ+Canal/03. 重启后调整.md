### yml 配置 mysqllog

- 启动后会变化
- 查询mysql最新后重新调整

~~~sql
-- 查看当前正在写入的binlog文件
show master status;
~~~

~~~yml
binlog:
  mysql:
    host: 127.0.0.1
    port: 3306
    username: root
    password: znkj123
    server-id: 1        # 确保在 MySQL 主库中唯一
    binlog-filename: DESKTOP-3CK8IIS-bin.000586
    binlog-position: 9988
~~~

### canal 配置 mysqllog

- 同上

~~~example/instance.properties
canal.instance.master.journal.name=DESKTOP-3CK8IIS-bin.000586
canal.instance.master.position=21298
~~~
